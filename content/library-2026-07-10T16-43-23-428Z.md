**The feeling:** Your Hub should feel like a warm, well-kept notebook — cream paper, clay ink, headlines with character, everything calm and reachable with one thumb. Authored, not engineered.

Mobile is the whole point; desktop just inherits the same warmth with more room.

## LOVABLE UI HANDOFF

### Palette
- Paper: `#F5EFE2` (cream)
- Ink: `#2A2320` (clay-brown, near-black)
- Accent: `#B45B3E` (terracotta)
- Muted: `#8A7E6E` (warm grey)
- Rule: `#D9CFBA`

### Type
- Display: a warm serif with character (e.g. Fraunces, Recoleta)
- Body: a friendly humanist sans (e.g. Inter, Söhne) at 16–17px, line-height 1.55
- Headlines set slightly larger than expected; body copy narrow (60–72ch)

### Layout
- Single column, generous side padding on mobile (20–24px)
- Cards are letter-like: soft radius (14–18px), thin 1px rule, no heavy shadow
- Icons/emoji sit at 32–40px, off to the top-left of each card

### Motion
- Tap targets 44px minimum
- Transitions 150–200ms ease-out; nothing bounces
- Modals slide up from the bottom on mobile, fade in on desktop

### Voice
- Sentence case everywhere. No shouting caps.
- One sentence per idea. Trust the reader.

### Do / Don't
- Do: cream backgrounds, warm rules, quiet accents
- Don't: pure white, drop shadows, gradients, purple
