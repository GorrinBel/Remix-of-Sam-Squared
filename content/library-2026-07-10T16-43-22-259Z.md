Most first sentences are just you filling the space with words, "In today's world…", "I just wanted to reach out…"

Write the paragraph, delete the first line, read it out loud - again. 

Nine times out of ten, your second sentence was the real opening.

If the first sentence isn't something you'd say to someone sitting across from you at your kitchen table, delete it.
