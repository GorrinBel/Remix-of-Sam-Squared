---
title: Writing Tip: Cut Your Throat-Clear
emoji: ✍️
category: library
updated: 2026-07-10
---
Cut your throat-clear.

Most first sentences are just you clearing your throat — "In today's world…", "I just wanted to reach out…".

Write the paragraph, delete the first line, read it again. Nine times out of ten your second sentence was the real opening.

If line one isn't something you'd say to one person across a table, cut it.
